# Privacy Policy for Plume / Plume 隐私政策

**Effective Date / 生效日期:** 2026-02-14

---

## English

**1. Data Collection**
Plume does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or user information. All data generated by the user remains strictly local on the user's device.

**2. Photo Library Access**
Plume requests access to your device's photo library (`NSPhotoLibraryAddUsageDescription`) solely for the purpose of saving the images or widgets you create within the app. We do not read, scan, or upload your existing photos.

**3. Third-Party Services**
This app does not use any third-party analytics or advertising services that track user activity.

**4. Contact**
If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact us at: flashbrick.lab@gmail.com

## 中文 (Chinese)

**1. 数据收集**
Plume 不会收集、存储或传输任何个人数据或用户信息。用户在应用内生成的所有数据均仅保存在用户本地设备上。

**2. 相册权限使用**
Plume 申请访问您的相册权限（`NSPhotoLibraryAddUsageDescription`），仅用于将您在 App 中制作的图片或小组件保存到本地相册。我们不会读取、扫描或上传您相册中现有的任何照片。

**3. 第三方服务**
本应用未使用任何会追踪用户行为的第三方统计或广告服务。

**4. 联系方式**
如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请联系我们：flashbrick.lab@gmail.com.
